Background


As Good hope Nabulagala Primary, we strive to ensure the success of all our pupils in the 21st Century global world based on the principles of Christian values. Good hope Nabulagala Primary School promotes a Christian-centered environment which fosters the total formation of the whole child. The school, in agreement with parents, prepares pupils to develop life skills that enables them to meet and manage 21st century global challenges.

The philosophy of Good hope Nabulagala Primary School is founded on the five core principles of God fearing, self-discipline, Respect for self and others, excellence in all aspects of life and Responsibility and with these principles, the members of the administration, staff and parents are committed to teach the whole child with love, self-discipline, responsibility , understanding, and compassion. Good hope Nabulagala Primary School‘s administration and staff are committed to academic excellence of every child. Good hope Nabulagala Primary School is Christian value-centered and teaches pupils by guiding them to share their God-given talents, treasure and time with their neighbors in a spirit of friendliness and togetherness. We are committed to promoting a culture of love for learning in order to prepare students as life-long learners and Change agents.
